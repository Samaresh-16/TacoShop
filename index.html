About the Little Taco Shop

The LTS is a family-owned business that has been serving the community for over 20 years. We are proud to offer a variety of delicious tacos, burritos, and other Mexican dishes. Our food is made with fresh ingredients and prepared with care. We are committed to providing our customers with great food and excellent service. Come visit us today and see why we are the best taco shop in town!
